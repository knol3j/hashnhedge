This decision comes as the popularity of cryptocurrency investments continues to grow.  **Key Points:**  * The SEC's approval allows crypto ETFs to create or redeem shares using existing holdings rather than cash. * In-kind creations and redemptions can reduce settlement risks and lower transaction costs for investors. * This development may encourage more institutional investment in cryptocurrencies, which could lead to increased market liquidity and stability.  **Takeaways:**  1. The SEC's approval of in-kind redemptions is a significant step towards increasing the efficiency and accessibility of crypto investments.
